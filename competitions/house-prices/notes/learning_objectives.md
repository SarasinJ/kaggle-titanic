# House Prices Study Notes

Use this space to capture objectives, experiments, and reflections specific to the Kaggle House Prices: Advanced Regression Techniques competition.

Suggested outline:
1. **Objectives** – What you want to learn or validate.
2. **Milestones** – Concrete checkpoints (baseline model, feature set, CV target, etc.).
3. **Experiment Log** – Bullet list of runs with key settings + outcomes.
4. **Reflections** – Observations, open questions, and next steps.
